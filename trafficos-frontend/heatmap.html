Violation density vs. congestion impact

Direct response to: "How can AI-driven parking intelligence detect illegal parking hotspots and quantify their impact on traffic flow to enable targeted enforcement?" — two independent signals plotted at the same point: raw violation volume (enforcement workload) and PCU-weighted flow impact (actual congestion cost). They diverge meaningfully — that divergence is the targeting signal.

#JunctionViolations SeverityPCU Score
Severity weights violation subtype (PARKING IN A MAIN ROAD / DOUBLE PARKING score higher than WRONG PARKING / PARKING ON FOOTPATH) — a junction with fewer total violations but more flow-blocking subtypes can outrank a higher-volume junction with mostly footpath parking.
